My Personalized Teaching Approach

I believe that every student can excel at math, but not every student learns the same way. My approach moves beyond rote memorization to focus on **conceptual understanding** and developing strong problem-solving habits.

  • **Diagnose:** We identify the root causes of difficulty, not just the symptoms.
  • **Customize:** Lessons are tailored precisely to the student's curriculum and learning style.
  • **Empower:** I foster independence by teaching students *how* to think critically, not just *what* to compute.
  • **Connect:** We relate mathematical concepts to real-world applications to make learning meaningful.
